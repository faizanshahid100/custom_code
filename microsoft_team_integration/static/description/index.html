Microsoft Teams Integration

Microsoft Teams Integration

This application introduces the functionality to schedule Odoo meetings using Microsoft Teams Office 365 Meetings through the Odoo Calendar.

Description

Description

Experience the seamless integration of Microsoft Teams with Odoo Calendar, it enables participants to join Microsoft Teams Meetings directly from Odoo. Meetings created in Odoo are seamlessly linked to Microsoft Teams, streamlining collaboration for virtual teams. With this integration, there's no need for separate sign-ins to Microsoft Teams Meetings as everything operates securely within Odoo.

Standout Features

Standout Features

  • Available on Community and Enterprise Editions on Odoo 16 and 17 version.
  • Effective and easy integration. Say bye to sending teams meeting link emails manually as Odoo Calendar will now do that automatically.
  • Single-Click meeting access. Join the Microsoft Teams meeting with a single click via the Odoo calendar.

Exploring the module: (How to)

Exploring the module: (How to)

  • Download and Install the module.
  • Go to Settings > Users & Companies > Companies > Company > Microsoft Team.
  • Enter the unique the application ID, Client Secret from Microsoft Azure account.
  • Contact your administrator for the Redirect URIs.

    • Creating a meeting link in Odoo Calendar

  • Check the box Add Team Meeting to create a new tab Team Meeting which will contain the Meet ID and Meet URL.
  • Participants can be added by typing or selecting from the drop down.
  • Clicking on the Email or SMS button to share the meet link with the participants.
  • Clicking on the JOIN TEAM MEETING button will take you directly to the meeting.
